        title: VEV733 Special Module on Leadership-I
---
Credits: 1 (0.5-0-1)

Department: National Resource Centre for Value Education in Engineering

This module will address the strong linkages between the personal values of an individual and the desirable qualities of a leader. Going beyond the theories, it will emphasize on the practical aspect of looking within as well as connecting to the outside world and hence developing the qualities of a leader.
