---
    title: TXL780 Principles of Characterization of Functional and Technical Textiles
---
Credits: 3 (3-0-0)

Department: Department of Textile and Fibre Engineering

Pre-requisite(s): EC 75 for TT1; None for others Characterization of low stress mechanical and transmission (heat and moisture) characteristics of functional textiles. Characterization of various technical textile materials: extreme heat, fire and cold protective clothing; geotextiles; filter fabrics; 'fiber reinforced composites; electromagnetic shielding textiles; compression bandages; ballistic protective textiles; UV protective textiles; Hygiene and medical textiles; Automotive textiles; etc. Analysis of test results.
