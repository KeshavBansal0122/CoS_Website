**Credits:** 1 (1-0-0)



**Prerequisites:** [[/Textile and Fibre Engineering/TXL232 | TXL232]] and EC 75

#### Description 
The course aims at introducing new or highly specialized technological aspects in fabric manufacture. The course topics and content are likely to change with each offering depending upon the current requirement and expertise available with the department including that of the visiting professionals.
