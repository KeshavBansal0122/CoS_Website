---
        title: VEV739 Special Module on Professional Ethics-I
---
Credits: 1 (0.5-0-1)

Department: National Resource Centre for Value Education in Engineering

This module will bring out the need for professional ethics as recognised by several professional bodies in the world through discussion of practical case studies and the underlying tenets of the code of conduct of professional bodies. The course will initiate discussion on reasons behind deviation from these tenets and the relevance of these tenets of professional ethics in the contemporary world.
