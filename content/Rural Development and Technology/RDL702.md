title: "RDL702 Karigar & Traditional Industries"
---
**Credits:** 4 (3-0-2)

#### Description
Introduction to traditional Indian arts and industry. Classification of artisans based of skills, Geographical distribution of skills and relation to sustainable development method. Traditional Wood working and products, Leather working and products, ceramic working and products, Jewellery working and principles, Home furnishing, stone working and concept of home decor, Miscellaneous karigar and products.
