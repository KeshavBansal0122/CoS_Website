title: "RDL725 Ecological Perspective of Growth and Development"

**Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)

#### Description
The concepts of ecological studies, valuation of environmental resources, their scarcities and optimal usage. Concept of Sustainability. Global environmental issues. Monetary assessment of environment: theories, policies and valuation methods. The growth and the environment: in search of the "optimal" scale of growth, Sustainable Development. Population, poverty and the environment.
