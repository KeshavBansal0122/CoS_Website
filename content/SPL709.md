        title: SPL709 Principles of Economics for Public Policy

Credits: 3 (3-0-0)

Department: School of Public Policy

Introduction to Economics; Supply, demand and markets; Theories of consumer demand and cost; Welfare economics and deadweight loss; Market failures and government policies; Market structure; Taxation and National accounting; Productivity and growth; Saving and investment; Unemployment; the Monetary System; International trade; Monetary and Fiscal Policy.
