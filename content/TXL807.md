Credits: 2 (0-2-0)

Department: Department of Textile and Fibre Engineering

A comprehensive literature review on a research topic of current interest or futuristic, pertaining to a textile process or product or technology. Student should perform a comprehensive literature review on a research topic of current interest or futuristic, pertaining to a textile process or product or technology. The student should give an outline of the review and get approval from the program coordinator for registration of this course. The student registered for this course should give one mid-term presentation followed by a final presentation before a committee constituted by the program coordinator.
