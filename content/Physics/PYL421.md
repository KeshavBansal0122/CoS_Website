#### Description
Basics of low dimensional structures, QD, QW, nanostructures for optical and electronic applications, QD lasers, detectors, SET, Carbon based nanostructures, CNT, CNT optical, electrical, mechanical, chemical properties, sensors, drug delivery, photonic crystals, GMR, nanostructured magnetism, hydrogen storage, nanoclays, colloids, nanomachines, organic and biological nanostructures.
