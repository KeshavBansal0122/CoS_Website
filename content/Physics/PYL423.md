**Credits:** 3(3-0-0)



**Prerequisites:** [[/PYL201 | PYL201]]

#### Description 
Basics of photovoltaics, Quantum confinement and plasmonics in photovoltaic devices, Nanorod solar cells, Principle of operation of hybrid and dye-sensitized solar cells, Nanoscale materials for improving thermoelectric figure of merit, Photoelectrochemical cells.
