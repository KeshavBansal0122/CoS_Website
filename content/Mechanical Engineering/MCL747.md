    title: MCL747 Design of Precision Machines

**Credits:** 3 (2-0-2)



**Prerequisites:** For UG:[[/AML140 | AML140]], [[/Mechanical Engineering/MCL111 | MCL111]], [[/Mechanical Engineering/MCL211 | MCL211]]

#### Description 
Fundamental concepts in precision design; design for stiffness; controlling Degrees-of-Freedom, exact-constrained design; design of elastic mechanisms/flexures/compliant mechanisms; friction, hysteresis and micro-slip; actuators and sensors for precision motion; materials selection in precision machine design; slideways for long range precision motion; and dynamics of precision mechanisms.
