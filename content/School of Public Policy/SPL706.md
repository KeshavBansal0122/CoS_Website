**Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)



#### Description 
Sustainable development and the SDGs; Historical perspective on processes of technology development to meet societal objectives; nature and dynamics of technological change; elements of innovation processes and perspectives on organizing for effective innovation (and role of key actors); case studies on role of STI in agriculture, health, energy, and environment; potential future role of STI in SD in India.
