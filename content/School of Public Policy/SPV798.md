#### Description
Several infrastructure sectors will be examined in detail, including roads and highways, ports, shipping, inland waterways, green public transport, logistics and civil aviation, For each sector, the need for specific policy interventions, creation of the appropriate enabling environment, public private partnership arrangements, stakeholder responses, implementation issues and technological innovations will be examined. The precise contents of each offering may vary.
