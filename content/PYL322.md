Credits: 3 (3-0-0)

Department: Department of Physics

Prerequisites: [[PYL201]]

Nucleation and growth, Basic principles involved in growth with controllable dimensions, Chemical and physical techniques for growth of nanoparticle, nanorod, ultrathin films, monolayer materials, multilayer structures, nanocomposite materials. Self organized growth on substrates and templates. Micro and nanoscale pattering techniques.
